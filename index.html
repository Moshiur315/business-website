Our Philosophy

A Love Letter to Local Farmers

Founded in 2014, L'Essence began with a simple belief: that the best ingredients shouldn't travel more than 50 miles to reach your plate.

Chef Julian Vance works directly with local growers to curate a menu that changes not by the season, but by the week, ensuring every bite is a reflection of the land’s current bounty.
